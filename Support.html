How does DriveHub verify listings? +
Every listing goes through a 50-point inspection by one of our certified inspectors. We verify the vehicle's condition, history, mileage, and documentation before it goes live. Sellers also go through ID verification so you always know who you're dealing with.
What is the 7-day buyer guarantee? +
If the car you purchased isn't as described in the listing — different condition, undisclosed damage, or inaccurate mileage — you can request a full refund within 7 days of delivery. Contact our support team and we'll handle the rest.
How does escrow payment work? +
When you pay for a car, your money is held securely in escrow — not released to the seller until you confirm receipt and satisfaction. This protects you from fraud and ensures the seller delivers exactly what was advertised.
How long does delivery take? +
Delivery timelines vary by location. Within Beirut and the Greater Beirut area, delivery typically takes 1–2 business days. Nationwide delivery to all governorates takes 2–5 business days via our licensed transport partners.
How do I list my car for sale? +
Listing is free and takes about 10 minutes. Create an account, complete ID verification, fill in your vehicle details, upload photos, and set your price. One of our inspectors will contact you to schedule the 50-point verification before your listing goes live.
What fees does DriveHub charge sellers? +
Listing is completely free. DriveHub charges a 2.5% success fee only when your car sells — no upfront costs, no monthly fees. Premium placement and featured listings are available for an optional fee.
Is the AI Price Predictor accurate? +
Our predictor achieves 96% R² accuracy and is trained on 300+ real Lebanese-market transactions. It's the most reliable price estimation tool for the local market, though actual prices may vary based on condition, negotiation, and timing.

For Sellers

SELLER GUIDE

Verify Your Identity
Upload a valid Lebanese ID or passport. Verification takes under 24 hours. Verified sellers get a badge on every listing — buyers trust you more.
Create Your Listing
Fill in all vehicle details: brand, model, year, mileage, engine, drive type, price, and location. The more detail, the faster your car sells. Add at least 8 photos — listings with photos sell 4x faster.
Schedule Inspection
A DriveHub inspector will contact you to schedule a free 50-point inspection. Once complete, your listing gets the "Verified" badge and goes live on the platform.

Sellers who respond within 1 hour are 3x more likely to close a deal. Keep your messaging active, answer questions honestly, and arrange viewings through the platform.

We provide all the documentation templates you need for the Mechanique transfer. Our team can guide you through the official process step by step.

Safety

REPORT AN ISSUE

Help us keep DriveHub safe. If you've encountered a fraudulent listing, a suspicious user, a payment issue, or any other problem — report it here. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

Suspicious Listing
Fake car, stolen photos, misrepresented condition, or scam pricing.
Fraudulent User
Fake seller account, identity theft, or someone impersonating DriveHub.
Payment Problem
Escrow issue, unauthorized charge, or a transaction that didn't go through.
Delivery Issue
Vehicle not delivered, wrong car received, or damage during transport.
Harassment
Abusive messages, threats, or inappropriate behavior from another user.
Other Issue
Something else that doesn't fit the categories above.
All reports are confidential. Our trust & safety team reviews every report within 24 hours. False reports are taken seriously and may result in account suspension.
