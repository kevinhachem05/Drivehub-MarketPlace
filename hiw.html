01

Sign up with your email and complete a quick phone verification. Unlock saved listings, messaging, and your purchase history. Takes under 3 minutes — no subscription or credit card needed.
Free to join

Filter listings by brand, model, year, price range, fuel type, and location. View full descriptions, high-resolution photos, and our verified 50-point inspection report for every car — all before you leave home.

Found something you like? Message our team directly through the platform to ask questions or confirm the car is available. Then book a time to visit our exhibition at your convenience — we'll have the car ready for you.

Inspect the Car in Person
Visit our exhibition and see the car for yourself. Take it for a test drive, go over the inspection report with our staff, or bring your own mechanic for an independent check. No pressure — we want you to be fully confident before you commit.

Once you're happy with the car, complete your payment in person at our exhibition. We accept cash (USD/LBP) and bank transfers. Our staff will walk you through the paperwork and ensure everything is in order before you sign.

Our team manages the full ownership transfer and Mechanique registration on your behalf. We prepare all required documents, accompany you through the process, or handle it entirely so you don't have to deal with the paperwork yourself.
