PURPOSE.

DriveHub was built to solve a broken market. We're Lebanon's first fully verified automotive marketplace — where every listing is honest, every transaction is secure, and every buyer finds the right car.

Our Mission

REDEFINING THE CAR-BUYING EXPERIENCE

We started DriveHub in 2020 with a single belief: buying and selling a car in Lebanon should be transparent, safe, and fast. No more shady deals. No more overpriced lemons. No more unanswered calls.

Today, DriveHub powers thousands of transactions per year — connecting real buyers with verified sellers across every governorate. We're not just a marketplace. We're the standard.

Our Journey

HOW WE GOT HERE

2020

Founded in Beirut

DriveHub launched as a simple listings board after our founder couldn't find a trustworthy platform to sell his own car. The first month saw 200 listings and zero scams — the mission was clear.

2021

Verification System Launched

We introduced our 50-point vehicle inspection program and seller ID verification — the first platform in Lebanon to offer this level of listing authenticity.

2022

Nationwide Coverage

Expanded beyond Beirut to cover all of Lebanon's governorates. Partnered with licensed transporters to offer door-to-door delivery for every sale.

2024

AI Price Predictor

Launched our machine-learning price estimator — trained on 300+ real Lebanese-market transactions — giving buyers and sellers an instant, data-driven market value for any vehicle.

2026

Lebanon's #1 Car Marketplace

DriveHub now leads the Lebanese automotive market with 48K+ active listings, a 4.9-star average rating, and a team of 40+ dedicated to making every transaction the best one yet.
