Account & Sign In
How do I create an account?+
Click Sign Up on the homepage. Enter your email address and a phone number for verification, set a secure password, and you're in — the whole process takes under 3 minutes. No subscription or credit card is required to browse or use the Price Predictor.
You only need to verify your identity when you're ready to make a purchase or list a car.
How do I sign in to my existing account?+
Click Sign In at the top right of any page. Enter your registered email and password. If you signed up with Google or Apple, use the matching button. You'll be taken directly to your dashboard.
I forgot my password — what do I do?+
Click Forgot Password on the sign-in page. We'll send a reset link to your registered email address. The link is valid for 30 minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new link.
Can I browse cars without an account?+
Yes! You can browse all listings, view car photos, read descriptions, and use the Price Predictor without creating an account. An account is required only when you want to contact a seller, save favorites, or purchase a vehicle.
Browsing Cars
What information can I see on a listing?+
Each listing shows:

— Location: the city/area where the car is located (e.g. Beirut, Jounieh, Saida)
— Description: seller-written details about the car's history, condition, and features
— Car details: make, model, year, mileage, fuel type, transmission, color, and engine size
— Verified inspection report: our 50-point checklist completed by a certified inspector
— Photo gallery: multiple high-resolution images of the vehicle
Seller contact details are hidden until you initiate a message through the platform.
How do I filter cars by location?+
Use the Location filter on the search page to select a governorate or specific city. You can also sort results by distance if you allow location access in your browser. Results update instantly as you adjust the filter.
How does DriveHub verify listings?+
Every listing goes through a 50-point physical inspection by one of our certified inspectors. We verify the vehicle's condition, mileage, history, and documents before the listing goes live. Sellers also complete ID verification, so you always know who you're dealing with.
Can I save listings to review later?+
Yes. Click the heart icon on any listing to save it to your Saved Cars list, accessible from your account dashboard. You'll also receive a notification if the price changes on a saved listing.
Can I request an independent inspection?+
Yes. In addition to our included inspection, you can request an additional independent report from a mechanic of your choosing. Arrange a viewing through the in-platform messaging system — never share personal contact details outside the app.
AI Price Predictor
What is the AI Price Predictor and how do I use it?+
The Price Predictor helps you estimate the fair market value of any car in Lebanon before you buy — so you know if a listing is priced fairly or overpriced.

To use it: go to the Predict Price page, select the car's make, model, year, mileage, and condition, then click Get Estimate. You'll get an instant price range with confidence indicators.
No account needed — the predictor is free and open to everyone.
How accurate is the Price Predictor?+
Our model achieves 96% R² accuracy, trained on 300+ real Lebanese-market transactions. It's the most reliable price estimation tool for the local market. Actual prices may vary slightly based on current condition, negotiation, and seasonal demand.
Can I use it to set a budget before I start browsing?+
Absolutely — this is one of its best uses. Run a prediction for the car type you want (e.g. 2019 Toyota Corolla, average condition, 80,000 km) and use the estimated range as your budget guide. You can then filter listings to that price range on the search page.
Does the predictor account for USD vs LBP pricing?+
Yes. The predictor outputs prices in both USD and LBP at the current market exchange rate. All DriveHub listings are priced in USD for consistency, with an LBP equivalent shown at checkout.

I have a dispute with a seller — who do I contact?+
Contact support immediately via Live Chat or email and mention "dispute" in your subject line. Our mediation team will step in and work with both parties to reach a fair resolution — typically within 48 hours.
How do I report a suspicious listing?+
Use the Report button on any listing page, or visit our Report an Issue page. Our trust & safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes immediate action if a listing is found to be fraudulent or inaccurate.
Safety & Trust
How does DriveHub protect me from scams?+
Every seller is ID-verified. Every listing is physically inspected. Our trust & safety team monitors the platform 24/7 for suspicious activity. Never pay or communicate outside the platform.
Is my personal data safe?+
Yes. DriveHub is fully compliant with data privacy regulations. Your personal information is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and only used to facilitate your transactions on the platform. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
What is the 7-day buyer guarantee?+
If the car you purchased isn't as described — different condition, undisclosed damage, or inaccurate mileage — you can request a full refund within 7 days of delivery. Contact our support team and we'll handle everything, including coordinating return transport if needed.
What should I do if I see a suspicious listing?+
Use the Report button on any listing page. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours and will take action immediately if a listing is found to be fraudulent.
